
COMPANY POLICIES



Vacation Policy

All full-time employees receive 15 days of paid vacation per year. Vacation requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance through the HR portal. Unused vacation days can be rolled over to the next year, up to a maximum of 5 days.



Work Hours

Standard work hours are Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Remote work is available on Wednesdays and Fridays with manager approval.



Benefits

Health Insurance: Medical, dental, and vision coverage starting on day one. Company pays 80% of premiums.



Retirement: 401(k) plan with 4% company match. Vesting period is 3 years.



Professional Development: Annual budget of $1,000 per employee for courses, certifications, or conferences.
